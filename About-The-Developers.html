ABOUT THE GAME:

This game was designed as part of an assessment for a programming course.
The inspiration came from the initial ideas of reversing gravity in some sort of flying
movement and spawning levels for scrolling.
From here we chose our ninja character and worked to develop the game from there.
Most graphics, audio and program was created for this game.
