How To ...
Save The Ninja


1: Follow any prompts on screen to further the game.

2: Press SPACE BAR to flip the ninja and his running gravity.

3: Avoid any obstacles and spikes to keep the ninja safe

4: Collect as many coins and maybe even some handy powerups!

5: Run as far as you can to try keep the ninja alive for as long as possible

6: Have Fun and Good Luck!
